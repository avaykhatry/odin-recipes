Recipe for Affogato


Description

An affogato is a simple, yet delicious Italian dessert consisting of a scoop of ice cream (usually vanilla) topped with a shot of hot espresso. The name "affogato" means "drowned" in Italian, referencing the ice cream being submerged in the espresso. It's a popular dessert and coffee drink, often enjoyed as a post-dinner treat or a way to enjoy a coffee fix.


Ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Gelato
  • A Shot of Hot Espresso

Steps

  1. A scoop of ice cream is placed in a glass.
  2. Then a shot of hot espresso is poured over it.
  3. Enjoy by sipping the espressow with a spoon as it melts the ice cream.
  4. Or eat it like a traditional dessert.
