Recipe for Cheesy Nachos with Salsa


Description

Cheesy nachos with salsa are a delicious and popular Tex-Mex dish that combines crispy tortilla chips with melted cheese, a vibrant salsa, and often other toppings. The nachos are typically made with tortilla chips layered with melted cheese, and sometimes cheese sauce, and then topped with salsa, jalapenos, onions, tomatoes, and other optional ingredients. The result is a flavorful and satisfying appetizer or snack.


Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Melted Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Toppings like Jalapenos, pickled onions, guacamole, sour cream

Steps

  1. First spread tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  2. Add salsa and grated cheese to the chips.
  3. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  4. Finally, serve hot with toppings like sour cream, guacamole, and any remaining salsa.
