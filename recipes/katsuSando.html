Recipe for Katsu Sando


Description

Katsu Sando is a Japanese sandwich consisting of a breaded and fried cutlet, usually pork or chicken, sandwiched between two slices of soft, crustless bread. The "katsu" part refers to the Japanese cutlet (katsuretsu), while "sando" is short for "sandwich".


Ingredients

  • White Bread (Ideally Japanese Shokupan)
  • Fried pork or Chicken Cutlet (Tonkatsu or Chicken Katsu)
  • Shredded Cabbage
  • Tonkatsu Sauce, and sometimes Kewpie Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Start by frying a breaded pork or chicken cutlet (katsu).
  2. Then, spread one slice of bread with mayonnaise and another with tonkatsu sauce.
  3. Place the katsu on the tonkatsu-sauced bread, add shredded cabbage, and cover with the mayonnaise-spread bread.
  4. Finally, wrap the sandwich and let it sit, then trim the crusts and cut it into pieces.
